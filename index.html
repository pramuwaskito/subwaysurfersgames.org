Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is a classic endless runner game. The game mission is to run as far as possible along the subway tracks to help Jake surf away escape from the grumpy inspector and his dog. Use all kind of cool power ups such as jet pack or hovering skateboard along the way while avoiding obstacles and surf on top of moving trains, so you don't get caught! Collect as many coins as you can during your escape, and boost your character with various power ups along the way!
