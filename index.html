Subway Surfers

Meet the graffiti artist Jake, an inspiring kid with solid skating and epic surfing skills! A grumpy inspector and his dog walk and surprise Jake while working on a masterpiece. The game mission is to help Jack surf away and to outrun the inspector, use all kind of cool power ups such as Jet Pack or hovering skateboard to run and surf on top of moving trains and metallic train tracks so you don't get caught! Subway Surfers is an amazing game with great graphics and endless adventures! Collect as many coins as you can during your escape, and boost your character with various power ups along the way!
